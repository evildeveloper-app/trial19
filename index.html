Disclaimers
Prizm is a personal finance tracker, not a financial adviser. Everything you see here is calculated from the entries you make, so treat it as a helpful picture rather than an official record. For decisions about investing, tax, credit or the law, check your bank statements and talk to a qualified professional.
Your data lives on this device and, if you connect sync, in your own Google Sheet. Prizm runs no servers and never sees your numbers. Keep your family code and password safe, since anyone holding both can open the shared ledger.
